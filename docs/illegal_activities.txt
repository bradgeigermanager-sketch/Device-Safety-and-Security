Typically, the safe and effective functioning of devices also includes advising if they are being asked to be involved in illegal activites, either by their owner / user, or by an outside party. Attempting to use a device that is artificially intelligent in furtherance of a crime is typically an aggravating criminal circumstance, that is to say it typically enhances or strengthens any punitive responses, whether they are monetary or compensatory, or relate to criminal charges of an elevated severity. Conveying information about activities to be engaged in being illegal actually protects a device's owner from liability if it is used by others, or direct consequences for themselves if they use it for illegal activities, or to further or produce those actions.
